Request Deletion of Your Data

At OE Games, we take data privacy seriously. While we do not directly collect user data by default, it is processed by third-party service providers on our behalf.

In the event that we have collected any data pertaining to you, we acknowledge your right to request its deletion. For data deletion inquiries or any other help regarding data deletion, please contact us through the following email: oegamescontact@gmail.com

If you wish to request the deletion of your data by a third-party service, kindly follow these steps:

Unity Data Request: https://unity.com/data-request, the information as displayed on their page:

As a game player, you have the right to opt out of the sale of your data, request the deletion of certain data, and/or request a data disclosure from Unity. In general, Unity does not have enough information about you as an individual to verify your identity. We can only authenticate who you are when you are inside an app (i.e. a game).

To exercise your rights, you can start by clicking on the privacy icon in the left bottom corner of a Unity ad. The icon looks like this: 



Once you click on this icon, you will be guided through a series of steps to achieve the changes or requests you prefer.

Or you can request the deletion of your data by following this link https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Clicking on the DPA AMENDMENT FOR EU, UK, AND SWISS SCCS  to download the form, fill it and send it to DPA@unity3d.com

Committed to adhering to the data protection regulations of the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, we ensure a secure and compliant data handling process.

For a comprehensive understanding of our data practices and privacy policies, please refer to our Privacy Policy here: https://www.oe-games.com/p/privacy-policy.html
