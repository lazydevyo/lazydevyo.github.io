Privacy Policy:

Data Collection and Use:

Google Play Games Services:

Our game integrates with Google Play Games Services to enhance your gaming experience. Through these services, our game can access your Google Play Games data to display and manage leaderboards, track achievements, display your friends list, and manage game activities including online multiplayer social activities. This data is processed within the app to provide features like leaderboards and achievement tracking.

Data Handling:


Google Drive API:
Our game uses the Google Drive API to create, edit, and delete game-save data directly on your Google Drive. This helps maintain your game settings and progress across devices.

Google Play Games Services:
We utilize Google Play Games Services to enhance your gaming experience by accessing data such as username, user ID, avatar image, scores, achievements, friends list, and game progress. This data is used to display leaderboards, your friends list and track achievements within the game. During multiplayer online sessions, users who are connected may access this information, including viewing your Google Play Games public account details.

Data Storage and Security:
We do not store or collect this data ourselves; it is processed directly through Google Play Services. All data handled by these services is stored and secured by Google.

Data Sharing:

We do not share your Google Play Games data with third parties; it is used solely within the game to improve your experience and facilitate multiplayer interactions.

However, We share your Google Play Games username, user ID, avatar, in-game jersey name, approximate location and IP address with the Photon PUN public cloud service to facilitate online multiplayer matches. This information is used to display player names and avatars during matches to other people connected to the same room. Photon PUN does not store this data; it is only used temporarily to enable online connectivity. Other than this, we do not share your data with third parties. The data accessed through Google Play Services is used solely within the game to improve your experience.

Advertisers and Third Parties:

Certain third-party entities collect information about your device, including unique device identifiers. This information may be shared with ad publishers and attribution companies. The purpose of collecting and sharing this information is to enhance ad placement tailored to your interests.

Online Multiplayer and In-Game Communication Policy:

We utilize Photon Cloud Services for multiplayer and chat functionality. During gameplay, temporary information such as public IP addresses, approximate location, and Lobby Names, which include your in-game player name (also known as jersey name), Google Play Games username, user ID and avatar may be used to facilitate player connections. Please note that this information is shared with other players online during a multiplayer session, it is only temporarily collected while you are engaging in online activity within our games.

You can find information on how to request the deletion of your data by visiting our website www.oe-games.com. Navigate to the "Help" section at the top right corner, then click on "Request Deletion of Your Data."
